Who is Evan Burrison?

I am Evan Burrison - a singer, songwriter and producer from Fort Erie Ontario, Canada. I specialize in recording and writing Pop music and I am currently a student in the Music Industry Arts Program at Fanshawe College. I have been producing and writing commercial pop music for 6 years, and have since then released 16 of my own projects and have written and produced an array of songs for other Canadian pop artists such as Ava Gingrinch, SYREN, and Matt Ballagh.
